Fashion and portrait photographer who likes to spend much of his time on taking Photos

I've always been a photographer first, long before I stumbled upon my Passion for modelling. It all started with taking some random photos, which was'nt good enough but worth the first Try when I was younger , untill the day I posed and discovered another medium to express myself.

Let Other's to capture my photo is one of the best feeling . specially , when it's clicked as expected. I feel strongly about making the photos of me that I can connect with. Even though it is the emotions of my subject that I'm documenting , I find a reflection of myself in them.

There's no surprise that modelling is love of my life , but traveling is also the blood in my veins. I'm endlessly inspired by nature and adventure. I love waking up to new horizons , making friends , and exploring the world.

Why I'm interested in modelling ?

I think that being a model is a great way to meet people from all over the world. Making contacts and getting to places I never thought I'd go. In the model world, everything is permitted. A world that always surprises you about how far you can go after your dreams.

How important is modelling education ?

I never did courses in anything. I've learned the hard way on my own and I believed in myself and I knew I could get there with perseverance and motivation. Education is very important to me even though I know there are a lot of models who have never heard of the concept pf education.

What do you think makes someone "fit" to be a model ?

I think that looking different from the rest is very important in modelling , but in my opinion , they are looking for models with character, not only with beauty. and I'm very sympathetic (necessary skill a model should pose)

Most of the goals I have set for myself as a model, I have achieved so far. basically , what this means is that from now on, I am ready to just appreciate whatever life throws at me.

Attitude is Everything To Me , because bodies are always different and actually there exists different types of modelling and ofcourse different types of models (skinny, fatty, older, young,)

lastly , I came from a very religious and warm home. I learned growing up that it is important to be nice to everyone , and smile.
